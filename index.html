About

Just wrapped up my bachelor's in Computer Science (Winter 2025) and dove straight into my Master's in Data Science at CUNY SPS (Spring 2026). My passion for data science grew from hands-on experience during my internships and the electives I took—now I'm all in. Can't wait to keep building my skills and get more opportunities to work in the field.

When I'm not in class, I'm deep in my projects. Wrapping up MISHI, my stray cat tracking app, and currently researching my biggest undertaking yet: 4SeamMetrics. This baseball analytics platform is my way of combining two things I love—watching baseball and working with data. I'm building it to handle large-scale datasets using cloud infrastructure, baseball APIs, LLM workflows, and custom statistical models. This is a long-term project that'll keep evolving as I grow my skillset, and honestly, I don't see myself ever stopping work on it. It's the perfect intersection of passion and practice.
